Who I am

I'm a Computer Engineering student at Vali-e-Asr University of Rafsanjan, passionate about the intersection of technology and design.

My journey started with HTML & CSS and basic JavaScript, then expanded into WordPress, UI/UX design with Figma, and now Flutter & Dart for mobile apps.

I believe good design is invisible — it guides people naturally without getting in the way.
